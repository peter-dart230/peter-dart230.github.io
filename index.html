My Poem:"My Alarm"

MY ALARM!

goes off at 8 AM

SO LOUD

it blares next to my bed

I STAND

UP TALL

to silence
its call

and back under the covers
I groggily crawl

I fall back to sleep
for just a minute or ten

Then at 10:30
my eyes open again!

I have class at 11!

I really must go!

I do this each morning,
I dont learn or grow

Maybe I am a sloth
Every morning I'm s l o w

my bed is far too comfy
for me to say NO!
